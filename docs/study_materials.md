# User Study Materials: Mocksy Evaluation

## 1. Study Overview
**Objective:** Evaluate the usability, perceived helpfulness, and latency tolerance of the Mocksy AI interview coach.
**Target Audience:** Computer Science students and entry-level software engineering job seekers.
**Methodology:** Unmoderated Remote Usability Testing (Asynchronous).
**Duration:** ~15-20 minutes (Self-paced).

## 4. Task Details (Self-Guided)
**Scenario:**
You are a final-year Student / job seeker preparing for an engineering interview at **Amazon**. You want to practice your "Leadership Principles" stories.

**Your Goal:**
Complete a full interview loop (Intro -> Question -> Follow-up -> Feedback) without any external help.

**Steps to Follow:**
1.  **Account:** Create a new account.
2.  **Config:** Start an interview with `Amazon` settings.
3.  **Speak:** Answer at least 2 questions. Try to be specific (STAR method).
4.  **Review:** Spend at least 2 minutes reading the generated feedback, specifically the **STAR Breakdown**.
